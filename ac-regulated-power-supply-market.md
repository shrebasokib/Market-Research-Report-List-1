<p><p>The AC Regulated Power Supply market features two primary types: non-contact and contact AC regulated power supplies. Non-contact AC supplies utilize electromagnetic induction to provide stable output voltages without direct electrical connection, minimizing wear and enhancing safety. In contrast, contact AC regulated power supplies involve direct electrical connections to deliver consistent voltage regulation, making them suitable for a variety of applications requiring precise power stability. Both types cater to industrial, commercial, and consumer needs, emphasizing reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
