<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave(SAW) Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave(BAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic wave sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors utilize surface waves that travel along the material’s surface, making them ideal for applications like wireless communication and sensing. BAW sensors, on the other hand, rely on bulk waves that propagate through the entire material, offering advantages in miniaturization and higher frequency applications such as pressure and temperature sensing in telecommunications and industrial sectors.</p></p>
