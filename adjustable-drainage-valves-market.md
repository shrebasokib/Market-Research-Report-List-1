<p><ul><li>Magnetic Adjustment</li><li>Gravity Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adjustable drainage valves market consists of two primary types: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment. Magnetic adjustment valves utilize magnetic forces to control drainage flow dynamically, allowing for precise control of water levels in various applications. In contrast, gravity adjustment valves rely on gravitational force to regulate drainage, providing a more straightforward mechanism for managing water flow. Both types cater to different operational needs, making them essential in industries such as agriculture, construction, and wastewater management.</p></p>
