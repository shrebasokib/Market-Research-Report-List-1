<p><p>The antirheumatics pharmaceutical market consists of several key types. Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) alleviate pain and inflammation without steroids, while Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs offer stronger effects for severe conditions by mimicking natural hormones. Slow-acting drugs, such as disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs (DMARDs), provide long-term relief by targeting disease progression. Immunological preparations, including biological therapies, modulate the immune system's response to minimize joint damage and improve overall function in rheumatoid arthritis and other rheumatic diseases.</p></p>
