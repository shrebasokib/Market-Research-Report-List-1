<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) bags market is primarily categorized into two production methods: Flat Film Strength Method and Tubular Quench Method. The Flat Film Strength Method involves creating flat sheets that are stretched in both dimensions, resulting in enhanced tensile strength and clarity. Conversely, the Tubular Quench Method produces bags from a tubular film, offering efficient material use and seamless construction. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including packaging for food, textiles, and consumer goods, ensuring durability and versatility.</p></p>
