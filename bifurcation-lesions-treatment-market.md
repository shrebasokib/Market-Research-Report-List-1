<p><ul><li>T Stent</li><li>Culotte</li><li>Crush</li><li>V Stent</li><li>Y Stent</li><li>Jailed Balloon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bifurcation lesions treatment market comprises various stenting techniques tailored to manage complex coronary artery blockages. T Stent involves single stent placement with a side branch; Culotte uses two overlapping stents for better coverage. The Crush technique involves stenting the main vessel and then the side branch. V Stent and Y Stent configurations also address bifurcations but differ in stent placement angles and strategies. Jailed Balloon technique involves inflating a balloon in the side branch while the main vessel remains stented. Each method aims to optimize blood flow and reduce restenosis rates.</p></p>
