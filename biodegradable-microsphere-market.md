<p><p>The biodegradable microsphere market includes various types of materials, each with unique properties. Polycaprolactone (PCL) offers good biodegradability and flexibility, making it suitable for medical applications. Polybutylene succinate (PBS) is known for its ease of processing and compostability. Polylactic acid (PLA) is widely used due to its versatility and strong compatibility with drug delivery systems. Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA) are produced from renewable resources, offering excellent biodegradability. Polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) has water solubility and biocompatibility, making it ideal for various applications.</p></p>
