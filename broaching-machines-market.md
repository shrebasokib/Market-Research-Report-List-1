<p><p>The broaching machines market features two primary types: vertical and horizontal broaching machines. Vertical broaching machines position the workpiece vertically, allowing for efficient cutting of intricate shapes and profiles, making them ideal for tasks requiring high precision. In contrast, horizontal broaching machines operate with the workpiece placed horizontally, typically suited for larger and heavier components, enabling batch processing. Both types play crucial roles in manufacturing, catering to various production needs based on the design and complexity of the parts being machined.</p></p>
