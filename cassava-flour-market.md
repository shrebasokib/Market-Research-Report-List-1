<p><strong>The Cassava Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sweet Cassava Flour</li><li>Bitter Cassava Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cassava flour market is primarily divided into two types: sweet cassava flour and bitter cassava flour. Sweet cassava flour is derived from sweet cassava varieties, known for their mild flavor and low cyanogenic content, making it suitable for various culinary applications, including baking and cooking. In contrast, bitter cassava flour comes from bitter cassava varieties, which contain higher levels of cyanogenic compounds and must be processed to reduce toxicity, often used in traditional dishes and regional cuisines. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and nutritional needs.</p></p>
