<p><p>The caulk market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications. Acrylic caulk is water-based, ideal for indoor projects and easy to paint over. Silicone caulk offers excellent flexibility and waterproofing, making it suitable for bathrooms and kitchens. Butyl rubber caulk provides strong adhesion and durability, often used for outdoor applications. Polyurethane caulk is highly resilient and versatile, effective for bonding and sealing in both interior and exterior settings. Other types may include latex and specialty blends for unique requirements.</p></p>
