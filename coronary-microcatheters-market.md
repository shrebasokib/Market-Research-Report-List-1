<p><p>Coronary microcatheters are specialized medical devices designed for accessing small blood vessels in the cardiovascular system. The precision and flexibility of microcatheters allow for enhanced navigability through the intricate vascular structure, thereby improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The Coronary Microcatheters Market is experiencing considerable growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period.
