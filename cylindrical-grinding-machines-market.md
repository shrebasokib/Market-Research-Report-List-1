<p><p>Cylindrical grinding machines are classified into three main types: CNC, manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled. CNC machines utilize advanced computer technology for precise automation and versatility, allowing for complex shapes and high production rates. Manually-controlled machines require operator input for adjustments and are often used for simpler tasks or smaller production volumes. PLC-controlled machines offer a balance between automation and manual operation, using programmable logic controllers for efficient task management while still allowing operator intervention for specific adjustments.</p></p>
