<p><strong>The Electrothermic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrothermic actuator market is primarily categorized into normally closed and normally open types. Normally closed actuators maintain a resting state that prevents fluid or gas flow until activated, ensuring safety and system stability. Conversely, normally open actuators allow flow by default and only close when activated, facilitating control in applications like HVAC systems. Each type serves specific operational needs, catering to diverse industrial applications, including automation and process control, where precise actuation is crucial for system functionality.</p></p>
