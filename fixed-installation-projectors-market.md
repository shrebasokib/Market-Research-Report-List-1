<p><p>The fixed-installation projectors market is segmented into foothold, wall-hanging, and roof-hanging types. Foothold projectors are positioned on stands or platforms, offering flexibility in placement. Wall-hanging projectors are mounted directly on walls, ideal for permanent installations with space optimization. Roof-hanging projectors are suspended from ceilings, providing unobstructed views and a clean setup. Each type serves specific installation environments and user needs, facilitating effective projection in educational, corporate, and entertainment settings while enhancing overall visual experiences.</p></p>
