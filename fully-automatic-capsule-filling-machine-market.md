<p><ul><li>Mechnical Vibration Filling Machine</li><li>Compression Filling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully-automatic capsule filling machine market is primarily categorized into two types: mechanical vibration filling machines and compression filling machines. Mechanical vibration filling machines utilize vibrations to facilitate the efficient and uniform filling of powder or granules into capsules, ensuring precise dosages. In contrast, compression filling machines utilize a compression technique to pack the material tightly into capsules, providing high density and minimizing powder dusting. Both types offer automation and efficiency, catering to diverse pharmaceutical and nutraceutical manufacturing needs.</p></p>
