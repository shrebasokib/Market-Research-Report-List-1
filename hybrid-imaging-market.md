<p><p>The hybrid imaging market comprises advanced diagnostic modalities that combine different imaging techniques for enhanced visualization and analysis of physiological and anatomical features. PET/CT integrates positron emission tomography with computed tomography, offering metabolic and structural data. SPECT/CT combines single-photon emission computed tomography with CT imaging, providing functional and anatomical information. PET/MRI merges positron emission tomography with magnetic resonance imaging, allowing for superior soft tissue characterization and improved diagnostics, particularly in oncology and neurological disorders. These modalities enhance precision in disease detection and treatment monitoring.</p></p>
