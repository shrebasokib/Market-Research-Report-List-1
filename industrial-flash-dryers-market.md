<p><p>The industrial flash dryers market includes two primary types: direct drying and indirect drying. </p><p>Direct drying involves the application of hot air to evaporate moisture quickly from materials, making it suitable for heat-resistant substances. In contrast, indirect drying uses heated surfaces that transfer heat to the material without direct contact with hot air, making it ideal for sensitive products. Both methods are employed across various industries to efficiently reduce moisture content in materials, enhancing productivity and product quality.</p></p>
