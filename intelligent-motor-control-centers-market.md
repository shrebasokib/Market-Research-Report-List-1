<p><p>Intelligent Motor Control Centers (IMCC) are categorized into low, medium, and high voltage types based on the voltage levels they manage. Low Voltage IMCC typically operates under 1,000 volts and is used for small motors and applications in industrial settings. Medium Voltage IMCC operates between 1,000 to 35,000 volts, often used in larger industrial processes. High Voltage IMCC surpasses 35,000 volts, catering to heavy-duty applications like power generation and transmission. Each type enhances efficiency, safety, and control in motor operations.</p></p>
