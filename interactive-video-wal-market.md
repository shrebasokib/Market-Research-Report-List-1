<p><p>The Interactive Video Wall market is primarily categorized into three types: LCD, LED, and DLP displays. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) walls are known for their vibrant colors and high resolution, making them suitable for indoor environments. LED (Light Emitting Diode) walls offer greater brightness and flexibility, ideal for various lighting conditions and larger sizes. DLP (Digital Light Processing) technology utilizes micro-mirrors, providing high contrast and excellent image quality, suitable for both large venues and immersive experiences. Each type serves different application needs.</p></p>
