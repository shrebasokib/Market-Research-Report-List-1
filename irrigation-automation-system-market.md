<p><p>The Irrigation Automation System market is divided into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic systems operate independently, utilizing sensors and controllers to monitor soil moisture and weather conditions, triggering irrigation when needed. This provides optimal water usage and reduces labor costs. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention, allowing users to control irrigation schedules while benefiting from automation features. Both types enhance efficiency, conserve water, and improve crop yield, catering to varying levels of user involvement and management preferences.</p></p>
