<p><strong>The Letterpress Print Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Glued Labels</li><li>Pressure Sensitive/Self Adhesive Labels</li><li>Liner-less labels</li><li>Multi-part Barcode Labels</li><li>In-mold labels</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Label</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The letterpress print label market comprises various types tailored for distinct applications. Wet glued labels are affixed using moisture, often seen on bottles. Pressure sensitive labels adhere with a backing, offering ease of application. Liner-less labels eliminate the backing for cost efficiency. Multi-part barcode labels feature layers enabling easy tracking and inventory management. In-mold labels, produced within containers, ensure durability and branding. Shrink sleeve labels conform to container shapes when heated, providing seamless graphics and coverage, enhancing product visibility.</p></p>
