<p><ul><li>High Temperature Resistance Type</li><li>Normal Temperature Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The masking film market comprises two primary types: high temperature resistance type and normal temperature type. The high temperature resistance masking films are designed to withstand elevated temperatures during processes like painting or powder coating, ensuring durability and protection without melting or deforming. In contrast, normal temperature masking films are suitable for ambient conditions and are commonly used for general protection in non-heat-intensive applications. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, balancing performance and application requirements.</p></p>
