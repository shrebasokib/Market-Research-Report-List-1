<p><p>The personal safety alarms market is segmented into active and passive alarm types. Active alarms require user initiation to trigger an alert, typically involving a button press or activation. These devices actively respond to emergencies, providing immediate notifications to nearby individuals or authorities. Conversely, passive alarms operate automatically, activating based on predetermined conditions such as motion detection or environmental changes. These alarms remain vigilant without user intervention, ensuring continuous monitoring and enhancing personal safety in various situations.</p></p>
