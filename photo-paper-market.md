<p><ul><li>Silver Halide Photographic Paper</li><li>Inkjet Photographic Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photo paper market primarily consists of two types: silver halide photographic paper and inkjet photographic paper. Silver halide photographic paper is traditionally used in film-based photography, featuring a light-sensitive layer that produces high-quality prints with rich detail and color. In contrast, inkjet photographic paper is designed for digital printing, utilizing various coatings to optimize ink absorption and drying time. Both types cater to different photography needs, with silver halide favored for archival quality and inkjet preferred for versatility and convenience.</p></p>
