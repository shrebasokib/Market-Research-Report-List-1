<p><p>The plastic fiber laser market is primarily segmented into Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. Continuous Wave Fiber Lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring steady, high-power output, such as welding and cutting plastics. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers deliver energy in short bursts, making them suitable for precision tasks like engraving or marking. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in plastic processing applications.</p></p>
