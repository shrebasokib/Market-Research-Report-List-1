<p><strong>The RF Power Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-based Meters</li><li>Diode Detector-based Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF power meters are essential tools for measuring radio frequency power in various applications. The market includes heat-based meters, which measure power by detecting the heat generated in a resistor when RF energy is absorbed, providing high accuracy across a broad frequency range. Diode detector-based meters, on the other hand, utilize semiconductor diodes to convert RF signals into a measurable DC voltage, offering faster response times and compact designs, making them ideal for portable and low-power applications.</p></p>
