<p><strong>The RF Tappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Up to 5 dB</li><li>5 to 10 dB</li><li>10 to 15 dB</li><li>15 to 20 dB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF tappers are classified based on their coupling loss, which determines the amount of signal diverted from the main path. The market types are categorized as follows: </p><p>- **Up to 5 dB**: Low loss, ideal for minimal signal degradation. </p><p>- **5 to 10 dB**: Moderate loss, suitable for applications with a balance between signal strength and tapping. </p><p>- **10 to 15 dB**: Higher loss, often used in more complex setups needing greater signal separation. </p><p>- **15 to 20 dB**: High loss, suitable for specific testing or monitoring applications where significant signal extraction is required.</p></p>
