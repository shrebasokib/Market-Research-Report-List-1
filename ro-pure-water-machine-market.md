<p><strong>The RO Pure Water Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Pure Water Machine</li><li>POE RO Pure Water Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO Pure Water Machine market is segmented into two primary types: Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) systems. POU RO machines are designed for individual use, typically installed at specific faucets or appliances, providing purified water directly where needed. In contrast, POE RO systems treat water at the entry point of a building, ensuring that all water supply is purified before distribution throughout the premises. Each type serves distinct needs based on application and usage.</p></p>
