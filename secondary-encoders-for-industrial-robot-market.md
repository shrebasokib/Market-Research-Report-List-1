<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the industrial robot market, secondary encoders play a crucial role in enhancing precision and control. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each point in their rotation, allowing for immediate detection of the robot's position, even after power loss. Conversely, incremental encoders track movement by counting pulses as the encoder rotates, which requires a known starting position to determine absolute location. Both encoder types are essential for optimizing the performance and reliability of robotic systems in various applications.</p></p>
