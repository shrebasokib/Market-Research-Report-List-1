<p><p>The semiconductor silicon epitaxial wafer market is categorized into two main types: P-Type and N-Type wafers. P-Type wafers, doped with elements such as boron, have an abundance of positive charge carriers (holes), making them essential for certain electronic applications. In contrast, N-Type wafers, doped with elements like phosphorus, have a surplus of negative charge carriers (electrons), facilitating efficient conductivity. Both types play crucial roles in the manufacturing of integrated circuits, solar cells, and various electronic devices, each catering to specific functionalities.</p></p>
