<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal IR Sensors market comprises two main types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection sensors utilize semiconductor materials to detect infrared radiation through photon absorption, providing high sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for applications like spectroscopy and gas analysis. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure temperature changes resulting from infrared radiation absorption, typically offering broader wavelength sensitivity and robustness. They are commonly used in surveillance, firefighting, and industrial monitoring, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness in various applications.</p></p>
