<p><p>The Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) service market primarily encompasses two main types: aortic stenosis replacement and aortic regurgitation replacement. Aortic stenosis replacement targets patients with narrowed aortic valves, improving blood flow and alleviating symptoms. In contrast, aortic regurgitation replacement addresses the issue of aortic valves that do not close properly, leading to backward blood flow and heart strain. Both market segments are driven by aging populations and advancements in minimally invasive cardiovascular technologies, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
