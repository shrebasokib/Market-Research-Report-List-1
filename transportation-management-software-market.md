<p><p>Transportation Management Software (TMS) is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise TMS requires installation on local servers, providing organizations with full control over data and customization, but often involves higher upfront costs and maintenance burdens. In contrast, cloud-based TMS is hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial investment. It allows users to access the system from anywhere, facilitates real-time updates, and reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure, making it ideal for many modern businesses.</p></p>
