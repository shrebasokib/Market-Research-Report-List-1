<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Dental Cleaner Machine</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Dental Cleaner Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic tooth cleaning machine market comprises two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic dental cleaner machines. Magnetostrictive machines utilize a magnetostrictive transducer to produce ultrasonic vibrations, effectively breaking down plaque and calculus on teeth. In contrast, piezoelectric machines rely on piezoelectric crystals to generate ultrasonic waves, providing precise cleaning with less heat generation. Both types enhance dental hygiene by improving cleaning efficiency, reducing the need for manual scaling, and promoting better patient comfort during dental procedures.</p></p>
