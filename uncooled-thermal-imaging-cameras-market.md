<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Mid Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Long Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Far Wave IR Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uncooled thermal imaging cameras are categorized based on their sensitivity to different infrared wavelengths. Short Wave IR cameras detect wavelengths from 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, suitable for applications like night vision and industrial inspections. Mid Wave IR cameras cover 3 to 5 micrometers, ideal for military and security use. Long Wave IR cameras operate between 8 to 14 micrometers, commonly used in building diagnostics and surveillance. Far Wave IR cameras extend beyond 14 micrometers, focusing on advanced scientific and meteorological applications, providing high-resolution thermal imaging.</p></p>
